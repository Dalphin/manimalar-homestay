About Us

Mani Malar Home Stay

Kodaikanal is a miniature paradise on earth and is often referred to as "Princess of Hill stations." It is tucked away among the Palani Hills in Tamilnadu, in South India, and is guarded like a prized jewel by the dark forests.

Our Homestay is the unique place to get a feel like of your home in Kodaikanal. which is situated in the entrance city of Kodaikanal. Its just a few minutes’ drive away distance from the Kodaikanal Lake and the city.

We offers an entirely different kind of experience customized to offer the guests an unpretentiously luxurious accommodation.The homestay is designed with nine family bathroom attached, a big living hall, kitchen, dining and a Veranda which provides a mesmerizing view of the enthralling beauty of nature.

This is complimented with a beautiful lawn in front of the homestay, where you can relax in the midst of garden surroundings and enjoy the great weather.The rooms are designed and furnished in such a way that you can enjoy the comforts of a luxurious home with modern facilities.

The homestay can accommodate a maximum of 20 persons and is a convenient and ideal destination for families and friends who wants to enjoy the privileges of royal living and rejuvenate their mind, body and soul. To match this, there is a team of dedicated staff who provide distinguished level of personalized service to every guest with utmost sincerity and we also serve fresh homely meals which include both veg and non veg which is a creative assortment of cuisines.

We also offer tailor made programs such as trekking, camping, nature tours and local sightseeing. Experience the comfort and joy of facilities & services at our Homestay and make your holiday a happy and memorable one.
